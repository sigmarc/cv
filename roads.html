Catalonia's Road and Street Network February 2015

Using CartoCiudad data I created this map with QGIS that displays the whole road and street network of Catalonia. We can observe the great urban footprint around Barcelona and the coastline aswell as the gaps all over rural and mountainous areas.

CartoCiudad information is published through open and free web services that comply with the Open Geospatial Consortium (OGC) standards and are implemented according to ISO and INSPIRE frameworks. The project is led and coordinated by the National Geographic Institute (IGN).
