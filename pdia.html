Client Distribution and Service Area March 2016

This map was created for a supermarket chain that wanted to display in a web-map their shopping centers and the service area for direct marketing campaigns. When zooming in we can see the location of around 315.000 loyalty card clients, thus helping the decisionmaking to keep or alter the distribution area. A search box was added to find the shops based on the layer attribute ID Store (e.g. 1079).
